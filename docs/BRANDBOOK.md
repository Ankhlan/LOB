# CRE Brand Book
## Central Risk Exchange (Централ Эрсдлийн Бирж)

---

## Executive Summary

CRE is Mongolia's first regulated cash-settled futures exchange. Our brand represents trust, transparency, and professional financial services in a market historically plagued by opacity.

**Mission**: Bring global markets to Mongolian investors through transparent, cash-settled derivatives.

**Vision**: Become the trusted gateway for Mongolian capital into global commodities and currencies.

## 2. Logo System

### Primary Mark
Pure typography: **CRE.mn**

No icons. No symbols. The domain is the brand.
Simplicity is elegance. Authenticity through restraint.

### Logo Files

| File | Usage |
|------|-------|
| logo-light-full.svg | Light backgrounds, print |
| logo-dark-full.svg | Dark backgrounds, web default |
| logo-light-icon.svg | Favicon, app icon (light) |

### Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "C" in CRE around all sides of the logo.

### Minimum Size
- Digital: 80px width minimum for full logo
- Print: 20mm width minimum for full logo
- Icon: 16px minimum (favicon), 24px recommended (app)

### Incorrect Usage
- Do not add shadows or effects
- Do not stretch or distort
- Do not use unapproved colors
- Do not add icons or symbols
- Do not use on busy backgrounds

## 4. Typography

### Primary Typeface
**Inter** — Designed for screens, excellent number rendering, full Cyrillic support.

| Weight | Name | Usage |
|--------|------|-------|
| 300 | Light | Logo, large headlines |
| 400 | Regular | Body text, paragraphs |
| 500 | Medium | Numbers, data tables |
| 600 | Semibold | Emphasis, buttons |

### Type Scale

| Level | Size | Line Height | Weight |
|-------|------|-------------|--------|
| H1 | 32px | 40px | 300 |
| H2 | 24px | 32px | 400 |
| H3 | 18px | 28px | 500 |
| Body | 14px | 22px | 400 |
| Small | 12px | 18px | 400 |
| Caption | 10px | 14px | 500 |

### Font Stack
`
font-family: 'Inter', -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', sans-serif;
`

### Number Display
- Use tabular figures for aligned columns
- Monospace for real-time prices
- Positive: Green (#98c379)
- Negative: Red (#e06c75), preceded by minus

### Mongolian Cyrillic
Full support for characters: А-Я, а-я
- Primary: Inter (native Cyrillic support)
- Fallback: Noto Sans (for extended characters)

## 5. Voice and Tone

### Brand Voice
- Clear over clever
- Direct over diplomatic
- Humble over boastful
- Mongolian over foreign

### Writing Guidelines

| Do | Do Not |
|----|--------|
| Use simple language | Use jargon without explanation |
| Be direct and honest | Over-promise or exaggerate |
| Respect the user's time | Add unnecessary words |
| Explain risks clearly | Hide fees or conditions |

### Example Copy

**Good**: "Your order was filled at 2,850.25"
**Bad**: "Congratulations! Your order has been successfully executed!"

**Good**: "Margin required: 500,000 MNT"
**Bad**: "Just a small deposit to get started!"

## 9. Motion

### Principles
- Subtle over dramatic
- Purposeful over decorative
- Fast over slow

### Timing

| Type | Duration | Easing |
|------|----------|--------|
| Hover | 150ms | ease-out |
| Transition | 200ms | ease-in-out |
| Modal | 250ms | ease-out |
| Page | 300ms | ease-in-out |

### Animations
- Fade in new content
- Slide modals from bottom
- Scale buttons on press (0.98)
- No bounce effects
- No spinning loaders (use skeleton screens)
